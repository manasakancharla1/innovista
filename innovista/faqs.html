Frequently Asked Questions

Can I check my registration details in the "Registrations" tab?

Absolutely! Head over to the "Registrations" tab, where you'll find all the updated details for the upcoming events. Ensure everything is in order before the big day.

How can I register for an event after reading guidelines and knowing the registration fee?

Simple! Navigate to the "Registrations" tab, choose your desired event, read the guidelines, and hit the "Register" button. You'll find the registration fee details right there for your convenience.

Can I customize my schedule based on the events I'm interested in?

While we don't offer customization options, rest assured that all scheduled events are carefully curated to provide valuable knowledge and opportunities. Attendees stand a chance to win contests, gain certificates, and enhance their LinkedIn profiles and resumes through their participation.

Where can I find detailed information about the club and the event venue?

Dive into the "About" tab for a comprehensive overview of Innovita Club and the venue details. Get to know us better and prepare for an exciting event!

What types of events are covered?

We cater to a diverse audience! From technical challenges to non-technical engagements, hackathons, webinars, and seminars, there's something for everyone. Explore the "Events" tab for upcoming activities.

How will I know the venue of the event?

No worries! As the event date approaches, we'll send you an email with all the details, including the venue. Keep an eye on your inbox for the announcement.

What is the cancellation and refund policy after making a payment?

Once payment is processed, we unfortunately cannot offer cancellations or refunds. Be sure to review the event details before completing your registration.

When will the rules for the
event be addressed?

Get ready! All rules and guidelines will be covered 15 minutes before the start of each event. Stay tuned for important announcements to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience.
